As they always say “ NEED IS THE MOTHER OF INVENTION “.

It all started a few years ago when I was out looking for some abayas and scarves that are pretty to wear and also have a good quality fabric. But it’s kinda hard to find any specific brands for Hijabis & Niqabis . So I started to design my own Abayas and scarves. I also had a passion for blogging so I started to make youtube videos where I teaches how to wear niqab and hijab with different styles. People kinda liked what I wore and I started getting alot of requests to sell my designs. That’s when things took a turn and I decided to launch my own abaya collections.

My aim is to inspire women and young girls who find it hard to fit in the immodest beauty standards of society. I believe that “ Modesty and fashion can totally go hand in hand. “

Dressing modestly isn’t looking outdated, it’s respecting yourself & looking elegant at the same time.

So,Abaya Store is a brand for all the women who are struggling to look beautiful and modest at the same time!

Your decision to wear a hijab deserves to be celebrated with products that genuinely make your life easier, make you feel more comfortable and confident and allow you to express your personal style. We take immense pride in using only the highest quality materials, meticulously constructed to last.

We provide you with high Abyas, Jilbabs, Kimono, niqabs, khimars & hijab blended up with pretty colors and elegants cuts. We have a wide range of , daily wear, semi formal and formal Abayas to slay any occasion modestly.
